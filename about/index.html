About Me

Edited: July 1st, 2023

theaxolotlmod is a mod project -- primarily aimed at Quilt. It was founded by me, as a last-resort attempt to fix Minecraft axolotls. Because Fabric is falling apart and Quilt seems to be better, I forked the Quilt template mod into a new mod, with the following ideas in mind:

  • Fixing where axolotls spawn
  • Adding Olms to the game
  • Adding a Mountain Lake Biome
  • Adding axolotl stuff
  • Fixing the axolotl model

theaxolotlmod has not grown very much since its inception, with relatively no support from the wider community.
