theaxolotlmod

theaxolotlmod is an open-source, me-driven mod originally
programmed with the Quilt mod-loader. By focusing on
axolotls, this mod aims to bring you axolotls that are both
more and less accurate to real axolotls

WIP

theaxolotlmod is currently in pre-pre-alpha Here be dragons -- there may be issues, but we'll fix them as quickly as we can!
If you'd like to help us test theaxolotlmod, please feel free to install it, try it, and let us know how you get on!
