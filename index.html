theaxolotlmod

theaxolotlmod is an open-source, me-driven mod originally programmed with the Quilt mod-loader. By focusing on axolotls, this mod aims to bring you axolotls that are both more and less accurate to real axolotls

theaxolotlmod is a mod that was born out of a need for change in the Minecraft axolotl ecosystem. I contain 0 prior experience with modding, providing a level of knowledge that helps me to make mistakes -- most of the time those mistakes are trying to understand what I'm doing. For more information on how I to do this, please see my about page.

WIP

theaxolotlmod is currently in pre-pre-alpha. Here be dragons -- there may be issues, but I'll fix them as quickly as I can!

If you'd like to help me test theaxolotlmod, please feel free to install it, try it, and let me know how you get on!

Caring

theaxolotlmod is me-driven, and couldn't exist without me. I care about my community; whether you're a member of my community spaces, an occasional contributor to one of my projects or simply a user: theaxolotlmod exists for your needs -- not in spite of them.
